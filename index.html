BIO

The Muffins, a musical group characterized by electro- energetic music.

TOUR SCHEDULE

The Muffins Band is group of 3 young people; Gian, Lucie and Marto from Kenya. Their debut single “Hold Me feat. tobyMac” landed them a nomination at the 2012 Grammy awards where they were the youngest nominee group. That year, they was also nominated for a Billboard Award and four Dove Awards, going home as the Dove Awards New Group of the Year.
